Research Profile

PhD candidate in applied mathematics working at the interface of optimal control, stability theory, and mathematical biology. My research focuses on chemostat models with perturbations — including mutation and horizontal transfer — using ODE and PDE frameworks. I combine analytical stability methods with numerical optimization techniques, with applications to natural resource management and biological systems.
